MAIL-IN REPAIRS

  • How does it work?

    • Mail us your repair and we'll get it fixed up for you!

  • How do I get started?

    • First, call us or email us a repair request containing the following information:

    1. Device model

    2. Issue

    3. Rush service needed or not

    4. Permission to erase device data if needed

  • Here are the contact info:

    • cheapphonerepairs.square@gmail.com

    (225)664-9046

  • What's next?

    • Wait for us to approve the repair and give you a repair quote.

    Once your repair has been approved, you can proceed with shipping your device to us.

    Your repair quote may change if the condition of the device is inaccurate or there are more possible damages to the device.

  • Where do I ship it to?

    • 2626 South Range Avenue, Suite 800,
    Denham Springs, LA 70726

  • Packing Instructions

    • 1. Pack your device carefully using bubble wrap.

    2. Include a printed copy of your request info along with your repair quote.

    3. Do not send us your case or any phone accesory.

    4. Do not include your simcard unless otherwise noted.

    5. Ship your device.

  • What happens next?

    • 1. We'll inform you via email or phone once we've received your device.

    2. We'll do an initial examination of the device before we proceed with the repair.

    3. Once we've determined that the device can be repaired, we'll inform you so that we can proceed with the repair.

    4. After the completion of the repair, you will be notified via email or phone if the device was fixed including other details regarding it.

    5. We'll send you an invoice via email to pay for the repair up the conclusion of the repair.

    6. Once the invoice has been paid we'll prepare your device for shipping.

    7. We'll inform you as soon as we ship the device back to you.

    8. For any questions and concerns after the repair, kindly contact us via phone or email.
